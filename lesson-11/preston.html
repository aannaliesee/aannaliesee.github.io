Preston Rodeo

Along with our three nights of rodeo, we have parades and a great downtown sidewalk sale. The sidewalk sale brings vendors from our local vicinity and from surrounding states. Delicious foods, drinks, sweets can be found as you stroll down Preston’s main street and Oneida street.
