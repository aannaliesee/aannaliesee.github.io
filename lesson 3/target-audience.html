Target Audience

Description

People who want to do some sort of recreational activity or sport and want to know if the weather will be appropriate.

Personas

  • Steven Johnson
  • 31 years old
  • Father and Husband
  • Usually uses mobile device to check
  • Favorite Sports: Golfing, Mountain Biking
  • Steven wants to go golfing and wants there to be no wind with clear skies. He particularly wants to know about the morning right after the sunrise.

  • Clara Hoffman
  • 24 years old
  • College Student
  • Uses mobile device to check weather in the evening
  • Favorite Sports: road biking, soccer
  • Clara checks weather last minute to see if it is safe to ride her road bike on the streets in the afternoon. She wants it be free from rain or snow, but doesn’t mind if it is on the cold side.

  • Hillary Smith
  • 80 years old
  • Retired and grandmother
  • Uses desktop to check the weather every morning
  • Favorite Sports: Swimming
  • Hillary swims every morning with her friends. She wants to make sure there is no thunder storms, but doesn’t mind the rain.

Scenarios

I want to go swimming in my local outdoor pool but it looks stormy outside. If it rains, will there lightning?

I am going to compete in a cycling race that will last all day. What clothes should I bring to be prepared?

I have asthma and need updated every morning to know if I should wear a mask or not. Even with asthma, I also like to go for long morning walks. How smoky is it outside?

I love golfing but don’t want to practice my golf swing with wind being a factor. When will the wind die down?

My bike is chained up outside by my apartment. Should I bring it inside so it doesn’t get weathered?

I want to go surfing but am worried that it will be too cold because we are going into winter. Is 6am too cold to go surfing?
